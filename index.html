Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a new tournament in the application?

    To create a new tournament, log in to your account, navigate to the "Create Tournament" section, fill in the necessary details such as name, date, and rules, and then proceed to set up the initial bracket.

  • Can I edit the tournament details once it has started?

    No, once the tournament has started, certain details like the name and rules cannot be edited to maintain fairness. However, you can update match results and progress teams or individuals.

  • How are matchups and results communicated to participants?

    Matchups and results are communicated to participants through the application. Participants can view their matchups and results in the "My Tournaments" section of the application. Notifications may include emails, in-app alerts, or SMS messages, depending on user preferences

  • Can participants view the complete tournament bracket?

    Yes, participants can view the entire bracket at any time to see their upcoming opponents, track progress, and stay informed about the tournament's status

  • Is there a feature for live score updates during matches?

    Yes, our application supports live score updates. Participants or organizers can input scores in real-time, and the bracket will be updated accordingly
